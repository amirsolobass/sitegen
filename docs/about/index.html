About Me

I'm Amir Solomon — a developer, musician, and sound designer based in Israel. I've spent the last few years learning to build software seriously, and the last fifteen years playing music professionally.

Development

I build across the stack: CLI tools, REST APIs, AI agents, browser games, and static site generators (including the one running this site). My main languages are Python, TypeScript, Go, and JavaScript. I'm drawn to projects where I have to understand how things work from first principles — no magic libraries, no scaffolds.

Most of my technical foundation comes from Boot.dev, where I worked through backend engineering, systems programming, databases, and more.

Skills: Python, TypeScript, Go, JavaScript, PostgreSQL, SQLite, Docker, Bun, Express, React, Drizzle ORM, REST APIs, Pub/Sub (RabbitMQ), AI/LLM tool use, static site generation

Music

I've been performing professionally for over 15 years. My work spans jazz festivals, studio sessions, teaching, and music production.

Live highlights: - Casa del Jazz — Rome, 2021 - +233 Jazz Club — Accra, Ghana - High-profile festivals and venues across Israel

Production: I hold a music production certificate from PointBlank Music School, and work across composition, arrangement, and sound design.
